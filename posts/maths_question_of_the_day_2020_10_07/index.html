Question of the day.

Average

Today our topic is average, solve the following questions.

Find the mode, median and mean of following:

a) 7, 11, 8, 11, 9

b) 9, 5, 13, 9

c) 5, 1, 8, 5, 3

d) 6, -1, -4, 0, 5

  1. The number of children in 8 families was 2, 1, 3, 4, 2, 3, 2, 1. Find the mode, median and mean.

  2. A cyclist travels for 2 hours at 20 mph and then for 1 hour at 15 mph. Work out his average speed for the whole jorney.

  3. The mean of 17, 37, 29, x, 15 and 19 is 24. Find the value of ‘x’.

  4. The age of my three children are 8 years 9 months, 5 years 4 months and 2 years 7 months. Find, in years and months their mean age.

  5. The mode of five numbers is 3, the median is 15 and the mean is 12. Find the numbers.

  6. The mean height of the 15 boys in a class is 162cm and the mean height of the 10 girls is 152cm. Work out the mean height of the 25 pupils.
